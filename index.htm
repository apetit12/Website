ABOUT ME

I am a research scientist at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, working on network design and schedule control for public transit systems. Using mathematical modeling and operations research techniques, I aim at designing reliable and cost-efficient systems to improve their level of service, and possibly increase ridership. The overall goal is to be able to provide a sustainable solution to reduce traffic congestion in urban areas through shared mobility.

I am interested in applications of machine learning, data science, and operations research in the broad fields of technology, e-commerce, or digital media to have a positive impact on the community.
